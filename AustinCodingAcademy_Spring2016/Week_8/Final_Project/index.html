After Hours

A Short Film

About The Film

Race is at the forefront of contemporary American discourse. It’s volatile and inflammatory, stirring tension and, most regrettably, inciting violence. We’re especially sensitive to appearances and perception in matters of race – perceived threats, perceived prejudices. After Hours is intended in part to show how these appearances are just that: appearances. They can deceive and mislead.
